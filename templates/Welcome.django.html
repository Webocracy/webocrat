REAL DEMOCRACY

Real democracy is not possible without a communication system capable of handling the challenges that arise when millions of voices need to be heard. With the use of the internet and new technology this is no longer just a dream.

TRANSPARENCY

Developing an open system is key to our success. We believe that any public system should be open-sourced, owned and controlled by the people that use it. This means no hidden tricks and no meetings with closed doors.

BLACK BOOK

Being open, online communication can be easily disturbed. Having a button for filtering out troublemakers, spammers or evil corporate/political interests saves valuable time and energy while discouraging similar actions.

TRUST NETWORK

A trust-based rating system instantly shows the real support any member of the group has. This means that you can give and take back the trust you gave to a person or entity. At any time. Just like in real life, you can choose to make this information public or keep it to yourself.

ORGANIC GROUPS

A natural organisational model, that better reflects the relationship between people and their collective goals, allows us to focus on the real issues we are facing, getting the hassle of organising communication out of the way.
